# Story Concept

## What to Include

### Core Premise
- What is this story about at the highest level?
- What's the central conflict or dramatic question?
- What makes this story worth telling?

### Story World
- When and where does this take place?
- What are the key rules or realities of this world?
- What's unique or compelling about the setting?

### Central Conflict
- What forces are in opposition?
- What's at stake?
- What prevents easy resolution?

### Thematic Territory
- What questions does this story explore?
- What ideas or tensions interest you?
- What should readers think about?

### Rough Arc
- Where does the story begin (situation, status quo)?
- What breaks or changes?
- Where might it end (emotionally, not plot details)?
