# **USAGE NOTES**

- **Arc States are REQUIRED for**: antagonists, mentors, close allies, love interests, major obstacles
- **Arc States are OPTIONAL for**: minor supporting cast, functional characters, brief appearances
- **Keep focus on FUNCTION**: Every trait should serve the character's narrative purpose
- **Behavioral over psychological**: Emphasize what they DO, not just what they think/feel
- **Consistency is key**: AI implementation needs clear, repeatable patterns
