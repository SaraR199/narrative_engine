## NPE Compliance Verification

### Section 1: Causality & Plot Logic ✅
- All plot movement from character action (investigation, confrontation)
- Supernatural abilities exact proportional costs
- Revelations emerge from physical evidence, mistakes, or confrontation
- Deceptions have clear motivations (Draven: protective control; Cassia: ideological conviction)
- No convenient plot turns

### Section 2: Character Action & Decision Physics ✅
- Decisions based on incomplete information filtered through wounds
- Protective withholding (Draven hiding bond) faces escalating consequences
- **NOW INCLUDES**: Misinterpretation through psychological lens (abandonment/obsessive)
- Characters can't choose better until confronting wound (V3 defining moments)

### Section 3: Dialogue Physics ✅
- **NOW INCLUDES**: Dialogue escalation stages mapped (Ch 20 Cassia confrontation)
- Information revealed through slips, interrogation, mistakes
- Crucial revelations actively resisted

### Section 4: Pacing & Time Mechanics ✅
- Time expanded for confrontations, supernatural experiences, choices
- Chapter endings shift stakes/revelations/threats
- **NOW INCLUDES**: POV switches at dramatic irony moments (Ch 7, 12, 17, 21)
- Emotional residue carries between scenes

### Section 5: POV & Emotional Logic ✅
- **NOW INCLUDES**: POV filtering through emotional wounds specified (abandonment/father's death/Sera's loss)
- **NOW INCLUDES**: Conflicting emotions present simultaneously (Ch 10)
- Physical sensations convey emotional states
- Internal narration contradicts external behavior when manipulating

### Section 6: Information Economy ✅
- Revelations through physical evidence investigation
- Memory recovery triggered by objects/locations, fragmentary
- Lies revealed through knowledge they shouldn't possess, contradictions
- **NOW INCLUDES**: Earlier evidence accumulation (Ch 12, 15, 17)

### Section 7: Genre-Alignment Rules ✅
- **CRITICAL FIX**: Romantic resolution (Ch 26) NOW occurs after external threat eliminated (Theron killed Ch 25)
- Mate bond complicates, doesn't solve (autonomy questioned throughout)
- Investigation uses mundane techniques on supernatural crimes
- Mystery parallels internal arc (proving crown truth = proving self-truth)
